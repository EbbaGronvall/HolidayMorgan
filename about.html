Who are we?

We are four musicans who met whilst studying music in Stockholm, Sweden. We noticed quickly that we had the same goal with our music and that is to entertain people.

Maja Hobring

Maja is our singer and our glue. She holds the group together and makes sure everyone on and off stage is having a good time. She may look intimidating with her tattoos but she is the sweetest and she will always make sure your needs are met.

Oscar Erixon

Oscar is our drummer and he is the engine of the group. He has a lot of energy that is contagious to everyone in his surroundings. He's also a total pro when it comes to setting up the stage and does this in no time.

Martin Norman

Martin is our guitarist that puts the glitter in the songs with his quick fingers on his precious guitar.

Albin Signell

Albin is our bass player and the newest member of our little group. While he is slapping the strings on his bass he will also show you his amazing dancemoves.
